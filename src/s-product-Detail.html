NEW

198 CORRISION-RESISTANT GRAPHITE GASKET

For Extreme High-Temperature Applications

198 is a high-purity graphite sheet gasket with no fillers or binders. 198 contains a corrosion inhibitor that makes it an excellent gasket for almost all environments. It is ideal for use in extreme temperature applications where metal reinforcement cannot be used.
